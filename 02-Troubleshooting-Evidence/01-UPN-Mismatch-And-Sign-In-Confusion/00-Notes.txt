Issue:
The user’s on-prem UPN and Entra UPN do not match.

UPN's:
AD UPN: psandoval@corp.test
Entra UPN: psandoval@vurichard420spam2outlook.onmicrosoft.com

Impact:
This can create confusion during sign-in because the user may expect the on-prem identity to work in the cloud, while Entra shows a different sign-in suffix.

Initial Interpretation:
This is not a system failure by itself. The mismatch exists because the AD domain and Entra tenant domain are different.
This creates a realistic hybrid identity support issue because users may not know which UPN should be used for cloud sign-in. 
In real environments, this is often resolved by adding and aligning a custom domain.
